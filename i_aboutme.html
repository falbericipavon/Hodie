About me

My name is Gaston Rey, I've been playing the guitar since I was 16, then I started playing the bass and once I dabbled in keyboards.

I was inspired by my own experiences on a spiritual or emotional level seeking to create atmospheres that represent a point of life. Oriented in styles such as rock, metal and ambient, with more than 20 years of experience in the music sector.

The timeline of my released albums is as follows: Sun January 2020, ZenThree August 2020 and RoboSapiens October 2020.

SUN

It's a concept, composed on a 20 minute bus ride in 2012, I still remember that rainy day and little hope when it crossed my mind that an EP with specific songs could be a great idea, the famouse less is more.

This album consists of three songs, SUN, RAIN and MOON. It evokes different moments in anyone's life, the famous ups and downs.
